What is Student Traveller?

Student Traveller is a travel blog dedicated to students within the age range of 18-25 years. The students are ideally undergraduate or postgraduate students, who have successfully finished their first semester or summer exam and are now on a long break. They now want to depart on an adventurous and cheap travel holiday to either Europe or Asia. However, they have no idea which country to travel to, how to travel there for the cheapest price, or what to do in that country.

Student Traveller aims to provide students with an insight to destinations in Asia and Europe, as those countries tend to be cheap yet provide students with the best experiences. The blog also exhibits the beautiful photography of the tourist attractions taken in those countries. Finally, the blog has a travel resources section that shows students how to backpack lightweight and efficiently, find the best flights and accommodation, and improve their photography.
